ABOUT
CONCEPT WEAVERS

Concept Weavers Pvt. Ltd. is the ultimate choice for all your trims and accessories for the garments industry. We at Concept Weavers Pvt. Ltd. are a leading Indian company with over 18 years of experience in the apparel and textile industry. We specialize in the development and manufacturing of garment accessories and fashion trims. Our mission is to deliver value to our clients through innovation, quality, and customized solutions.

We are a fully integrated and certified manufacturing unit offering a diverse range of trims and accessories for garments, footwear, bags, and more. Our product portfolio includes:

  • Webbings
  • Recycled & sustainable tapes (canvas, twill, grosgrain)
  • Knitted & woven elastics
  • Drawcords with a variety of aglets (metal, plastic, silicone)
  • Bungee cords, zipper pullers, tassels, belts, laces and more
  • Custom canvas and beaded bags for fashion, retail, and promotions
  • Fancy trims for sleepwear
  • Other specialized garment accessories

We proudly supply leading international brands like GAP, Old Navy, Tommy Hilfiger, Zara, Target, and more, across markets in Vietnam, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Egypt, Kenya, Jordan, the UAE, and beyond.
